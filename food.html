Beijing Roast Duck

Beijing roast duck is the city’s best-known signature dish. Served with crisp skin, tender meat, and thin pancakes, it is prepared with care and often presented as a special meal rather than an everyday quick bite. The dish has become a symbol of Beijing’s culinary reputation and remains a highlight for many visitors. Zhajiangmian & Noodles

Zhajiangmian is a classic Beijing noodle dish topped with rich soybean paste and fresh vegetables. It is simple, filling, and closely tied to everyday local life. Many Beijing families see noodles like these as comfort food because they are both practical and full of familiar flavor.
