One-Day City Highlights

Start with the Forbidden City or the Temple of Heaven, then move to a traditional hutong area for lunch and a slower walk. In the afternoon, visit one major scenic landmark and end the day with a classic Beijing dinner. This route works well for visitors with limited time who still want a clear first impression of the city. Three-Day Cultural Deep Dive

Day 1 can focus on imperial Beijing, including the Forbidden City and nearby city landmarks. Day 2 can explore food culture and traditional neighborhoods. Day 3 can be used for the Great Wall or the Summer Palace, giving the trip a balance of history, food, and landscape. Visitors are encouraged to explore both major landmarks and local neighborhoods to gain a fuller understanding of the city. Helpful travel notes

Beijing is easy to explore by subway, taxi, and walking routes within older neighborhoods.
